- Normally, players are expected to discard their known-trash cards before discarding their chop.
- By discarding her chop instead of the known-trash card, Alice is signaling that something is very wrong â€” specifically, that Bob's chop is critical or playable.
- Bob should permanently move his chop by one position, and write "cm" on his slot 4 to mark it as _Chop Moved_.
- Bob is not allowed to discard on this turn. Instead, he **must** give a clue.
