- Alice clues number 4 to Bob, which touches a green 4 on slot 5.
- Bob discards trash from slot 4.
- From Cathy's perspective, was Alice's clue a [_Reverse Finesse_](../level-2.mdx#the-reverse-finesse)? Why or why not?

<Question />

<TabItem value="solution">

- Yes, this is a _Reverse Finesse_.
- Green 4 is not critical, so the 4 clue cannot be a _Save Clue_. Thus, it must be a _Play Clue_. Cathy is promised a green 3 to make the green 4 playable.
