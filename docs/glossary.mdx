---
title: Glossary
---

This is a glossary of common terms that have specific meaning in the H-Group convention framework.

| Term                           | Definition                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            | Reference                                                                                                                      |
| ------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| _Bluff_                        | A special move where a player clues an unrelated _one-away-from-playable_ card to get the next player to blind-play their _Finesse Position_.                                                                                                         | [Level 11 - The Bluff](level-11.mdx#the-bluff)                                                                                 |
| _Card Notes_                   | Written notes on cards to track information such as card identities (e.g. "r2"), _Finessed_ cards ("f"), _Chop Moved_ cards ("cm"), or other useful information.                                                                                      | [Level 1 - Card Notes](level-1.mdx#card-notes)                                                                                 |
| _Charm_                        | A special move where the next player blind-plays their _Fourth Finesse Position_.                                                                                                                                                                     | [Level 23 - Charms](level-23.mdx#charms)                                                                                       |
| _Chop_                         | The rightmost unclued card in a player's hand.                                                                                                                                                                                                        | [Level 1 - Chop](level-1.mdx#the-chop)                                                                                         |
| _Chop Move_                    | A special move that indicates a player's _chop_ card is important and should not be discarded. The _Chop Moved_ card is treated as if it has an "invisible" clue.                                                                                     | [Level 4 - Chop Moves](level-4.mdx#chop-moves)                                                                                 |
| _Clued_                        | A property of a card. A card is considered _Clued_ if it has been given one or more positive clues from other players. A card remains clued until it is either played or discarded. (A card being Clued is the same thing as a card being _Touched_.) |                                                                                                                                |
| _Context_                      | All of the meta-information about the game state. Players should take into account the full context when determining what a clue means.                                                                                                               | [Level 12 - Context](level-12.mdx#context)                                                                                     |
| _Discharge_                    | A special move where the next player blind-plays their _Third Finesse Position_.                                                                                                                                                                      | [Level 16 - Discharges](level-16.mdx#discharges)                                                                               |
| _Double Discard Situation_     | A situation where the previous player discarded or misplayed a card and the current player could discard the other copy.                                                                                                                              | [Level 9 - Double Discard Situations](level-9.mdx#double-discard-situations--double-discard-avoidance-dda-severity-2-stalling) |
| _Dupe_                         | A property of a card. Short for _Duplicate_. A card is considered a _Dupe_ if two or more copies of the card are currently _Clued_.                                                                                                                   |                                                                                                                                |
| _Early Game_                   | The turns before any player has discarded their chop card. When this happens, the game transitions to the _Mid-Game_. Some clues change meaning after this transition.                                                                                | [Level 1 - The Early Game](level-1.mdx#the-early-game)                                                                         |
| _Efficiency_                   | A statistic of a game (i.e. "we need a lot more _Efficiency_ to win the game") or an evaluative quality of a move (i.e. "Alice's _Finesse_ was a 2-for-1"). Must be balanced with _Tempo_.                                                            | [Level 3 - Efficiency](level-3.mdx#efficiency)                                                                                 |
| _Ejection_                     | A special move where the next player plays their _Second Finesse Position_.                                                                                                                                                                           | [Level 16 - Ejections](level-16.mdx#ejections)                                                                                 |
| _Elimination Notes_            | If an important card is discarded, a player who does not see the other copy will normally assume they have it in their hand and write _Elimination Notes_ for that card.                                                                              | [Level 18 - Elimination & Elimination Notes](level-18.mdx#elimination--elimination-notes)                                      |
| _End-Game_                     | The period towards the end of a game when strategy shifts from _Efficiency_ to _Tempo_.                                                                                                                                                               | [Level 8 - The End-Game](level-8.mdx#the-end-game)                                                                             |
| _Finesse_                      | A move where a player gives a clue that causes another player to blind-play an unclued card from their _Finesse Position_.                                                                                                                            | [Level 1 - The Finesse](level-1.mdx#the-finesse)                                                                               |
| _Finesse Position_             | The leftmost unclued card in a player's hand.                                                                                                                                                                                                         | [Level 1 - Finesse Position](level-1.mdx#finesse-position)                                                                     |
| _Focus_                        | The specific card that a clue is primarily communicating information about. When a clue touches multiple cards, one card is considered the focus.                                                                                                     | [Beginner's Guide - Single Card Focus](beginner/single-card-focus.mdx)                                                         |
| _Good Touch Principle_         | A core principle stating that clues are only given to cards that will eventually be played.                                                                                                                                                           | [Level 1 - Good Touch Principle](level-1.mdx#good-touch-principle)                                                             |
| _Locked Hand_                  | A situation where a player's hand is fully clued (and/or _Chop Moved_), leaving them unable to discard.                                                                                                                                               | [Level 9 - Locked Hands](level-9.mdx#locked-hands-severity-3-stalling)                                                         |
| _Minimum Clue Value Principle_ | A core principle stating that all clues must get at least one previously unclued card played or saved.                                                                                                                                                | [Level 1 - Minimum Clue Value Principle](level-1.mdx#minimum-clue-value-principle)                                             |
| _Play Clue_                    | A clue that tells a player to play a card.                                                                                                                                                                                                            | [Beginner's Guide - Play Clues](beginner/play-clues.mdx)                                                                       |
| _Playable_                     | A property of a card. A _Playable_ card can be the focus of a _Play Clue_.                                                                                                                                                                            | [Level 1 - The Definition of Playable](level-1.mdx#the-definition-of-playable)                                                 |
| _Prompt_                       | A move that causes a player to play a clued card earlier than they otherwise would have.                                                                                                                                                              | [Level 1 - The Prompt](level-1.mdx#the-prompt)                                                                                 |
| _Save Clue_                    | A clue that tells a player to save a critical or important card for later.                                                                                                                                                                            | [Beginner's Guide - Save Clues](beginner/save-clues.mdx)                                                                       |
| _Save Principle_               | A core principle stating that the team must not let each other discard critical or important cards.                                                                                                                                                   | [Level 1 - Save Principle](level-1.mdx#save-principle)                                                                         |
| _Stalling Situation_           | A situation where a player may not have any normal _Play Clues_ or _Save Clues_ to give.                                                                                                                                                              | [Level 9 - Stalling Situations](level-9.mdx#stalling-situations)                                                               |
| _Tempo_                        | The speed at which cards are played. Must be balanced with _Efficiency_.                                                                                                                                                                              | [Level 3 - Tempo](level-3.mdx#tempo)                                                                                           |
| _Tempo Clue_                   | A clue that "gets" no new cards. Since they are not considered to be very good, they are only given in special situations.                                                                                                                            | [Level 6 - The Tempo Clue](level-6.mdx#the-tempo-clue)                                                                         |
| _Touched_                      | A property of a card. See the definition for _Clued_.                                                                                                                                                                                                 |                                                                                                                                |
| _Trash_                        | A property of a card. A card is considered _Trash_ if it can never play on the stacks or it is a _Dupe_.                                                                                                                                              | [Level 1 - The Definition of Trash](level-1.mdx#the-definition-of-trash)                                                       |
